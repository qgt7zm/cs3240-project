Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ page with a brief guide on how our web app works.

The app is designed to provide a safe and confidential platform where individuals can report concerns regarding student-athletes' well-being, including mental health, stress, and doping issues.

To submit a report, navigate to the 'Submit a Report' section of the app, fill out the form with the relevant details and any supporting documents, and click 'Submit'. Your report will be handled with discretion.

Once a report is submitted, it will be reviewed by an administrator. You can track the status of your report through the 'My Reports' section. If any further information or action is required, an administrator will reach out to you.

Yes, all reports are treated with the utmost confidentiality. We encourage users to report without fear of breach of privacy.

After submission, the report will be reviewed by administrators who may follow up for more information if needed. All reports are evaluated seriously and necessary actions will be taken to address the concerns.

Yes, you can choose to submit a report anonymously by not logging in. However, providing your contact information can be helpful if further information or follow-up is required.

You can report concerns related to any mental health issues, stress due to balancing academics and athletics, doping, or other ethical concerns that affect student-athletes.

Your report should include a clear and concise subject line, a detailed description of the concern, the category it falls under, and any supporting documents or evidence as files.

If you are a registered user, you can view the status of your report by navigating to the 'My Reports' section on the homepage.

Reports are reviewed by appointed administrators who are trained to handle sensitive information with discretion and professionalism.
